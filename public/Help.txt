Pop Pixie for macOS
-------------------

How to install:

1. Hold down the control key and click "Install Pop Pixie"
2. Click "Open"
3. When prompted, click "Open" again
4. This will open a Terminal window that will install Pop Pixie for you
5. When Pop Pixie has been installed, close the Terminal window
6. You will find Pop Pixie in your Applications folder (or in your Downloads folder if something went wrong)
7. Double-click Pop Pixie to open it
